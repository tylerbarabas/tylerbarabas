Music I've written...

Jack's Shack by Off The Fence

Off The Fence starts with a traditional acoustic sound - banjo, acoustic guitar, fiddle, upright bass - and adds a rock ’n’ roll attitude, resulting in an inspired and rambunctious take on old-timey music. The quartet embraces the Americana genre, with folk, blues, and country influences, as well as a healthy dose of bluegrass.

Check out the new album, Jack's Shack, available on iTunes, Spotify, CD Baby, and music outlets everywhere! Also, friend us on Facebook!

The Perfect Place

In this song, I tune the guitar to an open tuning, and play it in a fun, unconventional, percussive way laying flat on my lap. This was written as a song of appreciation for that person that makes me forget all my issues, problems, stresses of the day, and always helps to bring me to a place of joy.

I Know I'm Home

This song was written with two good friends of mine on a road trip to Austin, Texas. At the time we had all been living on the West Coast for a while, where our favorite fast food chain, Whataburger, isn't available. We let our stomachs guide our creativity, and this is what came out!

Alone With Your Sweater

I wrote this comedic tune after a hilarious conversation over drinks with my sister, in which we looked up all of our old exes on Facebook. The video was directed by Nate Cole Johnson, who did an amazing job with all of the angles and screenplay. I apologize in advance for the bad acting!
